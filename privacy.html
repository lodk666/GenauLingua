Privacy Policy for GenauLingua Bot

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Introduction

GenauLingua ("we", "our", "the bot") is a Telegram bot designed to help users learn German through interactive quizzes. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

When you use GenauLingua, we automatically collect and store:

User Information

  • Telegram User ID (unique identifier)
  • Username (if public)
  • First name (from your Telegram profile)
  • Interface language preference (Russian or Ukrainian)

Learning Data

  • Selected difficulty level (A1, A2, B1)
  • Translation mode preference (DE↔RU or DE↔UK)
  • Quiz statistics: answers, correct/incorrect counts, completion dates
  • Learning progress: words seen, words learned, streak days
  • Last activity date

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  1. Provide the service: Track your learning progress and personalize quiz content
  2. Improve learning experience: Use SRS (Spaced Repetition System) algorithm to show words based on your performance
  3. Display statistics: Show your progress, streaks, and quiz history
  4. Maintain service: Ensure the bot functions correctly

Data Storage

  • All data is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database
  • Data is stored on servers in the European Union
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • No data is shared with third parties

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as you use the bot
  • You can request data deletion at any time (see below)
  • Inactive accounts (no activity for 12+ months) may have data archived or deleted

Your Rights

Access Your Data

Contact us to receive a copy of all data we store about you.

Delete Your Data

Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us in the community chat: t.me/genaulingua_chat

We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Update Your Data

You can update your preferences (language, level, mode) directly in the bot settings.

Data We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Private messages or chat history
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Payment information (the bot is free)
  • Any data from other Telegram chats

Third-Party Services

GenauLingua uses:

  • Telegram Bot API (required for bot functionality)
  • PostgreSQL Database (data storage)

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

GenauLingua is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided data without consent, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance.

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted database connections
  • Secure server access controls
  • Regular security updates
  • No storage of sensitive personal information

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Community Chat: t.me/genaulingua_chat

All questions, bug reports, and data deletion requests are handled through our community chat.

Legal Basis (GDPR Compliance)

For EU users, we process data based on:

  • Consent: You choose to use the bot
  • Legitimate Interest: Providing and improving the learning service
  • Contract: Fulfilling the service you requested
